詳細の長い説明文
Happy Daily Journal is a gentle one-line diary app that helps you make every day a little happier.
It is designed for self-kindness, not pressure. Even short notes are enough to keep going.
With cute characters by your side, daily journaling feels light and enjoyable.

Key features
・One-line daily journal
Write your mood or one small win in seconds, even on busy days.

・Helpful reminders
Gentle notifications help you keep your journaling routine without stress.

・Daily positive message
Start your morning with a short uplifting message and reset your mindset.

・Character and wallpaper customization
Personalize the app so it feels truly yours.

・Cute badge rewards
Keep using the app and collect badges that show your progress.

・Sound effects and voices
Turn on sounds and voices to make your daily routine more fun.

Great for people who want to
・build a simple journaling habit
・improve self-kindness and consistency
・stay motivated with cute characters

Recommended daily flow
・Morning: read a positive message
・Night: write a short one-line diary
・Break time: enjoy mini games and character care
