詳細の長い説明文
Happy Daily Journal is a gentle companion app that helps you praise yourself and make each day a little happier.
Write a short daily note, check your daily fortune, and build a routine you can actually keep.

Key features
・One-line daily journal
Quickly record your mood and what you achieved today.

・Daily fortune check
Start the day with a light, positive prompt.

・Character care
Take care of your character and enjoy daily progress.

・Mini-games for a mood refresh
Short games for quick breaks and stress reset.

・Badges, voices, and personalization
Collect rewards as you continue and make the app feel like your own.

Great for people who want to
・build a simple journaling habit
・improve self-kindness and consistency
・stay motivated with cute characters

Why this works for self-care
・Small daily actions
With a one-line diary and mood log, you can keep a daily journal habit without pressure.

・Character growth motivation
As you continue your habit tracker routine, your character growth and rewards make progress feel visible.

・Gentle emotional reset
Daily fortune and mini games help you reset your mood and return to your day with more balance.

Recommended daily flow
・Morning: check your daily fortune
・Night: write a short one-line diary
・Break time: enjoy mini games and character care
