Starting the Development Diary

November 22, 2025 Development Diary

Introduction

Since starting TakeKApp, I have released various apps. I have developed apps that make users' daily lives a little more enjoyable, including Fun Topics, Absolute Happiness Fortune Telling, Fun Statistics, Multi-functional Dice, My Dreams 100, and AwaAwaPon.

I decided to start this development diary to share the development process, technical learnings, and daily insights. I will update it irregularly about initiatives leveraging my expertise in statistics × app development × AI/DX.

About TakeKApp

I work as a freelance AI/DX consultant and IT engineer, specializing in data analysis and app development. I hold a Statistical Certification Level 1 and develop mobile apps primarily using Flutter.

Currently, I work 3-4 days a week while working on personal development in my spare time. I became independent as a freelancer in August 2025. I will fully launch my freelance activities leveraging my expertise in statistics × app development × AI/DX.

What I'll Share in This Development Diary

In this development diary, I will share the following content:

  • App Development Process: The development process of new apps and improvements to existing apps
  • Technical Learnings: Technical insights and tips about Flutter, statistics, and AI/DX
  • Daily Insights: Insights gained through development, and thoughts on marketing and monetization
  • Freelance Experience: Preparation for independence and working as a freelancer

Future Outlook

I aim to update about once a month to improve the transparency of the development process and strengthen communication with users. I also hope to deepen exchanges with others working on app development through sharing technical learnings.

Thank you for your continued support.
