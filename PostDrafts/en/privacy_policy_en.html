Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Post Drafts (the "App") considers the protection of user personal information as an important responsibility and handles it appropriately according to the following privacy policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Created Within the App

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Memo content (text, titles)
  • Tag information
  • Registered hashtag information
  • App settings
  • Translation history

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following information:

  • App usage statistics (crash reports, performance data)
  • Device information (OS, app version)

2.3 Payment-Related Information

When using the Premium Plan, the following information may be collected:

  • Purchase history (via App Store/Google Play Store)
  • Subscription status
  • Payment-related error logs

This information is used for proper payment processing and support provision.

3. Purpose of Information Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing app functionality
  • App improvement and optimization
  • Bug fixes
  • User support

4. Third-Party Disclosure

The App will not provide user personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • When user consent is obtained
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect the life, body, or property of the user or third parties

5. Translation Feature

The App's translation feature uses Google Translate API. Text to be translated is sent to Google but is immediately deleted after translation is complete.

6. Data Storage

Memos and settings created by users are stored in the device's local storage. This data is retained until the app is uninstalled.

7. Data Deletion

Users can delete data through the app's settings. Additionally, uninstalling the app will delete all data.

8. Security

The App implements appropriate security measures to properly protect user personal information.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be changed as necessary. When changes are made, users will be notified within the app.
